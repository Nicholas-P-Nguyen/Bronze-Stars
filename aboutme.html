I joined the Marine Corps in 2016-2020 with a determination to transform my life and give back to the country that took in my family during the Vietnam War. We faced numerous financial hardships which took me away from my studies, after barely graduating high school with a 2.4 GPA in 2014, I worked and struggled through a year of community college which ended with a disheartening 1.0 GPA.

My decision to join the Marine Corps marked a pivotal moment in my life as it reshaped my prespective , instilling in me a strong sense of discipline, curiosity, and problem-solving abilities. Thanks to the G.I. Bill, I saw chance to redefine my educational path. Leveraging the skills veveloped during my serice, I excelled academically, and more importantly, proving to myself that I could be sucessful in higher education. This success spurred me to reach even higher, leading me to apply to some of the nation's top universities. Today, I am proud to be a student at Princeton University,
